DTS - Disciple Training School

Nowadays in nearly all cultures, young people have felt difficulty in discovering God’s call for their lives. There are so many opportunities to serve and so many giftings to be developed that many people simply become overwhelmed by the choices. The Disciple Training School is designed to help you discover your calling as a Christian. It is also the gateway for those who wish to be involved in missions through MissionNow and become an effective disciple of the Lord Jesus Christ.

DTS is not just an academic course where you can learn new things and develop knowledge, it has an emphasis on applying Biblical principles and developing a heart of faith. It is for Christians of all ages and cultures who wish to fulfill God’s call on their life. The course lasts for five months. For the first three months you will live on the MissionNow campus. The final two months are spent doing a practical outreach either in Brazil or another country.

The practical phase of the course gives you valuable experience in the field and will open your vision for missions and other possibilities to serve. At MissionNow Recife we receive students from Brazil and other nations and this creates an excellent opportunity for cultural exchange.

This Discipleship Training School is registered with the University of the Nations and can serve as a pre-requisite for all other major U of N schools together with formal MissionNow registration to either become a staff member or apply for other schools.

SOE - School of Evangelism

The great commission was given by Jesus almost 2000 years ago but the work is still not finished. The SOE – School of Evangelism seeks to prepare workers for the harvest described in Matt 9:37 ‘The harvest is plentiful but the workers are few.

SOE will give you the vital tools and skills to effectively relate the gospel to other cultures and societies around the world. The school focuses on the characteristics and ways of God, (Nature and character) and is designed to help you become an effective and powerful communicator of God’s truth.

The School is divided into two phases: a lecture phase of 3 months and a practical phase of 2 months. The outreach phase of this school encompasses intensive practical application of evangelism ministry and leadership training. The school is a registered University of the Nations (UofN) school, and qualifies for 20 credits upon completion.

The eight weeks of the practical are designed to expose you to the various needs of different people groups in this world, and help you see them as the Father does, ministering to them the love of Jesus. Students and staff usually pray together about the location options for the practical.





CRS - Children at Risk School

With an average of seven million street children in Brazil and millions more around the world, this course is designed to train people to reach at-risk children and bring the knowledge of God’s love to them.

During this course, you will be challenged to plan a program to reach children at risk. There are 12 weeks of classes with experienced teachers, who work with both social action and counseling. The training then offers a further 12 weeks of internship in our projects, so that the student can be accompanied and guided while getting practical experience.

TIf you are thinking about starting a social action project with children in your community, this may be your opportunity to find out how to get started amid so much need. This course is registered at the University of the Nations with 24 credits and is bilingual in Portuguese and English. The pre-requisite for entry is to have successfully completed a DTS, and be able to provide us with a recommendation from both your pastor and YWAM leader.
